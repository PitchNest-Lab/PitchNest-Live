## Delete account — what to tell stores

**Apple:** In-app deletion at Settings → Account → Delete Account. Web backup: `/delete-account`.

**Google Play Delete Account URL field:**
```
https://YOUR_DOMAIN/delete-account
```

That page lets users enter email + password to permanently delete their account without reinstalling the app.

**What is deleted:** Account, profile, pitch sessions, decks, recordings, and associated cloud files (within ~30 days).

**What is not auto-deleted:** Active Apple/Google subscriptions—users must cancel in device subscription settings.
